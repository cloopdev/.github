# Cloop

### Meet your autonomous engineering team

> Approve the plan. Review the PR. Get out of the way.

---

Cloop is an autonomous engineering control plane that turns your Linear issues into merged pull requests — with planning, execution, verification, and delivery handled end-to-end.

Four loops run continuously:

**⚡ Build** — You define what to build. Cloop plans, executes, verifies, and delivers a clean PR. You approve the plan and review the result. Everything in between is autonomous.

**🐞 Heal** — Automatically watch for and fix production. When something breaks, it investigates, builds a fix, and opens a PR with video proof of the fix validated in a real environment.

**🛡 Defend** — Every PR is scanned for CVEs before it ships. Loose version ranges are pinned. Critical findings go straight to the queue and get fixed like any other task.

**👻 Monitor** — A lightweight server agent lives inside the perimeter and watches what external monitors can't see, acts within safe boundaries, and auto-generates your status page from what it actually knows.

Every customer runs on a dedicated instance. Your code, your secrets, your context — fully isolated.
